### Usage

#### Code examples

- `as="a"`

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
```jsx
<Link href="https://example.com/">
  Read more about our products
</Link>
```

- `as="button"`

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
```jsx
<Link
  as="button"
  onClick={onClick}
>
  Check our offer
</Link>
```

- with a meaningful label

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
```jsx
<Link
  href="https://example.com"
  aria-label="Read more about our products"
>
  Read more
</Link>
```

- with an underline to indicate it's a link

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
```jsx
<Link
  href="https://example.com"
  underlined
>
  Privacy policy
</Link>
```

- disabled

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
```jsx
<Link
  as="button"
  onClick={onClick}
  disabled
>
  Check our offer
</Link>
```
